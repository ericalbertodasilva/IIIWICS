III International Workshop of Sucroenergetic Chain

It is with great pleasure that the “Luiz de Queiroz” College of Agriculture (ESALQ/USP), along with The University of Georgia (UGA-USA), Sugar Processing Research Institute (SPRI-USA), University of Washington, Piracicaba Technological Park and the Brazilian Institute of Bioenergy and Agribusiness host the "III International Workshop of Sucroenergetic Chain”, which will take place at the Piracicaba Technological Park, from July 25th to 26th 2018.

Round Tables

Speeches

Feedstocks

Energy Production

Industrial Processes and New Products

Policy, Management and Sustainability

EVENT PROPOSAL

The Event aims to spread new technologies, to discuss the main current challenges and to present trends by gathering professional who work in the productive chain of the sucroenergetic sector and related areas.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Professionals who work in the sucroenergetic sector, executives, producers, researchers, students, consultants and both private and public authorities.
